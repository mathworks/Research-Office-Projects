The Research Office Projects page is a rapid-publication channel from the MathWorks Advanced Research & Technology Office. It provides early previews of new algorithms, experimental capabilities, and work that extends beyond the functionality and examples shipping with MATLAB® and Simulink®. The projects span a growing range of domains, including physics-based optimization, computer vision, robotics, and sensor simulation, where MATLAB and Simulink can address complex research problems.

Each project featured here has been developed with the help of agentic AI workflows, using MATLAB MCP Server, MATLAB Agentic Toolkit, and Simulink Agentic Toolkit.
