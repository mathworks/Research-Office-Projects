This repository is a rapid-publication channel for the MathWorks Advanced Research & Technology Office. Its purpose is to quickly expose gap capabilities, prototype missing workflows, and demonstrate advanced uses of MathWorks tools that go beyond what ships in standard product examples. Every project here was developed using agentic workflows — AI-driven iteration loops powered by the MATLAB Agentic Toolkit and the Simulink Agentic Toolkit — enabling a single engineer to go from concept to fully documented, ready-to-run package in days rather than weeks.

The projects span a growing range of domains — from physics-based optimization and computer vision to robotics, sensor simulation, and beyond — showcasing how MATLAB® and Simulink® can tackle research-grade problems end-to-end. Each entry is self-contained with its own documentation and parameters, so you can clone and run immediately. New projects are added regularly as new gaps are identified.
